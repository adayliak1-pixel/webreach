Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common roofing questions

How long does a typical roof replacement take? +
Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on the size and complexity of your roof. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
What warranty do you offer? +
We offer a 10-year workmanship warranty on all installations, plus manufacturer warranties on materials. Full details are provided in your contract.
Do you handle insurance claims? +
Yes, we handle the complete insurance claim process including documentation, communication with your insurer, and coordination of the work.
Are you available for emergency repairs? +
Absolutely! We offer 24/7 emergency service for urgent roofing issues. Call us anytime and we'll prioritize your situation.
How much does a roof inspection cost? +
Roof inspections are completely free for new customers. If you decide to move forward with work, we'll credit the inspection fee toward your project.
What roofing materials do you recommend? +
We work with premium materials from top manufacturers. During your consultation, we'll recommend options based on your budget, climate, and aesthetic preferences.
Are you licensed and insured in Cleveland? +
Yes. We are fully licensed, bonded, and carry comprehensive liability and workers' comp insurance to ensure you and your property are 100% protected throughout the project.
